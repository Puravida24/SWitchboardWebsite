## Capability Names + Descriptions

### Naming Options
| Engine | Option A (Technical) | Option B (Benefit) | Option C (Operator) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Data Enrichment | Real-Time Intelligence | Signal Reception |
| 2 | Risk Segmentation | Predictive Scoring | Caller Identification |
| 3 | Quality Scoring | Intelligent Segmentation | Line Management |
| 4 | Decision Engine | Precision Routing | Connection Routing |
| 5 | Performance Analytics | Closed-Loop Attribution | Call Analytics |

### Best Descriptions (one per engine)
1. **Enrichment:** "Every incoming opportunity is matched against 343 million consumer records, appending 85+ data attributes in real time."
2. **Scoring:** "Proprietary ML models assign a conversion probability score to every opportunity in under 5 milliseconds, enabling instant decisioning."
3. **Segmentation:** "Opportunities are automatically categorized into buyer-defined tiers based on score thresholds, geography, and policy type."
4. **Routing:** "Scored and segmented opportunities are distributed to the highest-value buyer using configurable routing rules and real-time auction logic."
5. **Analytics:** "Every touchpoint tracked and visualized: conversion rates, buyer performance, revenue attribution, and pipeline health — all in real time."
