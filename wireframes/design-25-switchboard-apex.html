Platform Capabilities

One Platform. Complete Control.

No competitor does all five. The Switchboard is the only platform that enriches, scores, segments, routes, and analyzes — in a single pipeline.

Data Enrichment

Append 40+ data points to every opportunity in milliseconds. Phone validation, address verification, demographic scoring, and intent signals — all before routing.

Risk-Intelligent Scoring

ML-powered scoring models trained on millions of insurance transactions. Every opportunity gets a quality score, fraud probability, and conversion likelihood in real time.

Smart Segmentation

Auto-segment by line of business, geography, risk profile, and buyer readiness. Create custom segments with drag-and-drop rules — no engineering required.

Intelligent Routing

Route each opportunity to its highest-value buyer based on score, capacity, bid price, and historical performance. Supports ping-post, direct post, and real-time bidding.

Revenue Analytics

Real-time dashboards with source ROI, buyer performance, conversion funnels, and revenue attribution. Know exactly which sources and routes generate the most revenue.

Seamless Integration

Connect to any CRM, dialer, or downstream system. RESTful APIs, webhook events, and pre-built integrations get you live in days, not months.

Process

From Ingestion to Revenue

Four steps. Milliseconds. Every opportunity maximized.

01

Ingest

Data flows in from any source — web forms, APIs, call tracking, aggregators — through a single unified endpoint. No custom integrations needed.

02

Enrich + Score

Instantly append 40+ data points. ML models score for quality, fraud risk, and conversion probability. Every opportunity is profiled in under 12ms.

03

Route

Intelligent routing matches every opportunity to the highest-value buyer. Supports ping-post, real-time bidding, and weighted distribution.

04

Analyze + Optimize

Real-time dashboards reveal source ROI, buyer performance, and revenue attribution. Continuous feedback loops automatically optimize routing rules.
