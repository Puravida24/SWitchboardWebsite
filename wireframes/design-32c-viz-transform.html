The Experience.

Insurance is not a data problem. It is a judgment problem. Every carrier, every agency, every MGA operates in a world where the difference between a profitable book and a failing one comes down to knowing which opportunities deserve attention — and which do not.

We built The Switchboard because we’ve sat on both sides of the table — writing insurance AND buying the clicks that generate it. From that seat, the gap between marketing and underwriting wasn’t theoretical. It was expensive. So we closed it.

The Intelligence.

  1. 01

    Enrich

    Every incoming opportunity is instantly augmented with firmographic, demographic, and behavioral data drawn from 343 million records. You see the full picture before the first conversation.

  2. 02

    Score

    Our proprietary risk-scoring models — trained on two decades of insurance outcome data — assign a precision score to every opportunity. High-value prospects rise. Low-quality noise falls away.

  3. 03

    Segment

    Opportunities are automatically grouped by line of business, geography, policy type, and predicted lifetime value. Your team spends time on the right accounts, not all accounts.

  4. 04

    Route

    Intelligent distribution sends each scored, segmented opportunity to the right person at the right time. No manual sorting. No cherry-picking. No wasted motion.

  5. 05

    Analyze

    Real-time dashboards reveal which sources, segments, and agents convert — and which do not. The data loop closes. Your strategy sharpens with every cycle.
