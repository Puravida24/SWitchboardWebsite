The Company

Apex Insurance Group is a mid-size agency with 85 agents across 12 locations in the Southeast, specializing in personal auto and homeowners insurance.

The Challenge

Apex was spending $180K/month on leads with no risk intelligence. Close rates were declining, CPA was rising, and loss ratios were trending in the wrong direction.

• 60% of leads never quoted
• No visibility into lead risk profiles
• Carriers complaining about loss ratios

The Solution

The Switchboard implemented real-time Equifax enrichment, risk segmentation scoring (A-F tiers), carrier appetite matching, and dynamic routing.
