Platform Capabilities

Five connected engines.

Each engine works independently. Together, they form an intelligence layer no one else in insurance can match.

01

Data Enrichment

Append 140+ attributes to every record in real time. Property data, demographic profiles, behavioral signals, and risk indicators — all verified, all instant.

02

Risk Scoring

Proprietary ML models assign a 0-100 quality score to every opportunity. Trained on millions of outcomes, refined daily. Know before you buy.

03

Smart Segmentation

Automatically cluster opportunities by risk profile, geography, coverage type, and intent signals. Every segment maps to a buyer preference.

04

Precision Routing

Match every scored opportunity to the best-fit buyer in under 5ms. Weighted round-robin, priority tiers, and cap management — all configurable.

05

Live Analytics

Real-time dashboards tracking conversion rates, revenue per record, buyer performance, and margin trends. Export anything. Alert on everything.
