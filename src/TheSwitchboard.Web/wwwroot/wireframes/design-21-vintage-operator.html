The Process

From signal to
revenue in seconds

Every opportunity flows through four precision stages. Like a vintage switchboard operator connecting the perfect call.

1

Ingest

Prospects flow in from any source -- web forms, API partners, call centers, aggregators. Every signal is captured and timestamped.

2

Enrich & Score

Data enrichment appends 40+ attributes. Our scoring engine evaluates risk quality across 12 weighted factors in real time.

3

Match & Route

Intelligent matching pairs every opportunity with the ideal buyer based on line of business, geography, capacity, and risk appetite.

4

Deliver & Track

Sub-second delivery via API, CRM push, or real-time notification. Full attribution tracking from first touch to bound policy.
