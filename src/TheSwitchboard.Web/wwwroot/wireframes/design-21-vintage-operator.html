The Platform

Five engines, one operator.

01

Enrichment

Raw data arrives incomplete. Our enrichment engine appends 40+ verified data points in real-time — demographics, property records, credit indicators, policy history — transforming fragments into complete customer profiles.

02

Scoring

Our ML models evaluate every opportunity across 200+ weighted signals. Each prospect receives a composite quality score, intent score, and predicted conversion probability — before it ever reaches a buyer.

03

Segmentation

Scored opportunities are automatically sorted into dynamic buyer segments based on geography, line of business, risk profile, and buyer appetite. The right opportunity finds the right buyer — every time.

04

Routing

Like a master switchboard operator connecting calls, our routing engine delivers each opportunity to the optimal buyer in under 5 milliseconds. Priority queues, weighted distribution, and real-time capacity balancing ensure zero waste.

05

Analytics

Every connection generates intelligence. Track conversion rates, buyer performance, source quality, and revenue attribution in real-time. The data that flows through The Switchboard makes it smarter with every transaction.

The Process

The operator at work.

1

Ingest

Raw opportunity data flows in from publishers, forms, calls, and APIs. Every signal is captured.

2

Process

Data is enriched, validated, and deduplicated. Fraud signals are flagged. Quality is assured.

3

Score & Match

ML models score each opportunity. Buyer matching algorithms find the optimal connection.

4

Deliver

Qualified, scored opportunities reach the right buyer in <5ms. The connection is made.
